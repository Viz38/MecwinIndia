Introduction

This document outlines the warranties and service provisions for both on-site, off-site, and unconditional warranties associated with our Motors, Pumps and Controllers.

Scope of Service

We provide a range of services for PMC, including but not limited to:
• Installation and setup of motors and controllers.
• Troubleshooting and diagnostics for motor/Pumps/controller faults.
• Repairs for defective or damaged equipment.
• Replacement of faulty parts or components.
Service will be provided either on-site (at the customer’s location), off-site (at a designated service centre), or under unconditional warranty (which includes no-cost repairs or replacements regardless of fault).

Warranty Types
On-Site Warranty

On-Site Warranty applies when services are provided at the customer's location. This warranty includes the following:

  • Coverage Period: 60 months from the date of Invoice.
  • Service Coverage: Any defect in materials or workmanship found in the PMC during the warranty period.
  • Labour and Travel Costs: Includes labour to diagnose and repair defects and travel expenses for our technicians to visit your site
  • Exclusions:
  • o Damage caused by misuse, neglect, or improper installation.
  • o Issues arising from incorrect wiring, overloading, or modification of the motor or controller.
  • o Damage from external factors (e.g., power surges, floods, fire).
  • o Wear and tear, consumables, or normal aging of parts.
On-Site Warranty Service Process:
• To initiate an on-site service request, customers must contact customer care to register the complaint.
• A technician will visit the location to assess and repair the issue.
• If the problem is covered under warranty, repairs will be performed at no additional cost. Non-warranty issues will be billed at the standard service rate.

Off-Site Warranty

Off-Site Warranty applies when the motor/pump/controller is brought to an authorized service centre for repairs. This warranty includes:
• Coverage Period: 60 months from the date of Invoice.
• Service Coverage: Covers manufacturing defects or material faults.
• Parts and Labour: We will cover the cost of parts and labour required for repairs.
• Shipping Costs: The customer is responsible for shipping the equipment to the service centre. Return shipping will be covered by us if repairs are covered under warranty.
Off-Site Warranty Service Process:
• Customers must contact us to arrange the return of the motor or controller to the service centre.
• Upon receipt, we will diagnose the issue and notify you of the required repairs. If the issue is covered under the warranty, no charge will apply.
• After repair, the motor or controller will be returned to the customer at no additional shipping cost (if within warranty).

Unconditional Warranty

Unconditional Warranty offers the highest level of service and is provided for a set period after purchase, regardless of the cause of failure. This warranty covers:

  • Coverage Period: 60 months from the date of Invoice.
  • No-Questions-Asked Replacement: If the motor or controller fails, regardless of the reason (except for intentional damage), we will replace it with a new or refurbished unit at no cost to the customer.
  • Replacement Place: Customer must visit nearest service centre to get replacement of defective unit.
  • Parts and Labour: All parts and labour necessary for the repair or replacement are covered.
  • Exclusions:
  • o Damage due to user neglect, intentional misuse, or abuse.
  • o Equipment that has been altered, tampered with, or repaired by unauthorized personnel.
  • o Equipment damaged due to natural disasters, fires, or other catastrophic events.
Unconditional Warranty Service Process:
• Customers must notify us of the failure during the warranty period.
• We will issue a replacement or provide repair services at no cost, depending on the situation.
• The defective unit needs to be returned to us before the replacement is provided. We will cover return shipping for the defective unit.

Customer Responsibilities

To ensure the smooth processing of warranty claims and service requests, customers agree to the following responsibilities:

  • Proper Use and Maintenance: Customers must use and maintain the motor/controller according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  • Provide Accurate Information: When making a warranty claim, customers must provide the serial number, purchase date, and a description of the issue.
