    8. Permissions We Request

    Our app requires the following permissions:

    • Internet: To access our services.
    • Vibrate: To send notifications.
    • Send SMS: This is used to remotely control water pumps. When a farmer sends a text message from their registered phone number, the IoT device receives and acts on these commands. This feature is essential for the app's primary functionality of managing water pumps remotely.
    • Write External Storage: To download the data of the pump, if required by the user.
    • Read External Storage: To upload images.
