Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to farmers' details, service engineers, distributors, and their contact information. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy. The data collected is also provided to the government to facilitate user subsidies.



Permissions, Core Functionality, and Compliance with Google Play Policies

Our app requires SMS permissions for:

Device Automation: This permission is essential for sending SMS commands to control water pumps remotely from the farmer's registered phone number to the IoT device's registered phone number.

We request these permissions during the login process, ensuring users provide explicit consent. This ensures users understand why the permission is needed and how it supports the app’s functionality.

Necessity:
We request permissions that are essential for the core functionality of the app. The SMS permission is crucial for sending commands to IoT devices to control water pumps.

User Consent and Transparency:
Users are informed about the necessity of SMS permissions during the login process and must provide explicit consent to proceed. This ensures users understand why the permission is needed and how it supports the app’s functionality.

Data Usage:
The data collected via permissions is used strictly for the purposes described in the policy, including providing data to the government to facilitate user subsidies. Personal data is not sold or shared for unrelated purposes. If future updates introduce new uses of data, users will be informed, and their consent will be obtained.

Data Security:
We use encryption and secure servers to store user data, ensuring it is protected against unauthorized access. Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and they are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Regular Reviews and Updates
Our privacy policy and app functionalities are regularly reviewed and updated to comply with Google Play’s latest policies. Any changes to permissions or data usage are clearly communicated to users.



Detailed Information on Permissions

SMS Permissions:
These are necessary for the primary function of remotely controlling water pumps. We use SMS commands sent from the farmer's registered phone number to the IoT device’s registered phone number.

Location Data:
To provide precise control and monitoring of water pumps, location data is collected. This data helps in mapping the pumps and ensuring accurate service delivery.

Contact Information:
Collecting contact details of farmers, service engineers, and distributors allows us to provide timely support and updates regarding the service.

Device Information:
Information such as IMEI numbers and device specifications helps in identifying and managing the devices connected to our service.
