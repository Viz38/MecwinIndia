Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13/04/2024

1. Introduction

Mecwin Technologies ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.


2. Data Collection

For Farmers:


  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Aadhar Number
  • Address
  • IoT Device Details (Mecwin Pump details)
  • Distributor Name

    • For Service Engineers:


  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Aadhar Number
  • Employee Number
  • Distributor Name

    • For Distributors:


  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Addressr
  • Business Name
  • GST Number

    • 3. Data Usage

    The collected data is used for:


  • Submission to government agencies for subsidy purposes.
  • Improving our services.
  • Analytics.

    • 4. Data Sharing


    We share data with government agencies to facilitate user subsidies.


    5. Data Storage and Protection


    User data is stored in a PostgreSQL database on an Azure server, protected by Azure's security measures. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.


    6. User Data Management


    Users can edit their data but cannot delete or opt-out as it is required to be retained for compliance with government mandates.


    7. User Data Management


    Our app integrates with OpenStreetMap for location services.


    8. Permissions We Request

    Our app requires the following permissions:


    • Internet: To access our services.
    • Vibrate: To send notifications.
    • Send SMS: To turn water pumps on or off. Remote control of the pump is implemented by sending a text message from the farmer's registered phone number to the IoT device's registered phone number.
    • Write External Storage: To download the data of the pump, if required by the user.
    • Read External Storage: To upload images.

    9. Children's Privacy


    Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us. If We become aware that We have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, We take steps to remove that information from Our servers.
    If We need to rely on consent as a legal basis for processing Your information and Your country requires consent from a parent, We may require Your parent's consent before We collect and use that information.


    10. Contact Information


    For privacy-related inquiries, please visit our website at mecwinindia.com.


    11. Consent


    Before collecting any personal data, we will request your explicit consent. This will be done through a clear and unambiguous consent dialog that requires affirmative action (such as tapping to accept).


    12. Data Retention Policy


    We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and as required to comply with legal obligations, including government mandates. Users cannot request the deletion of their data due to these legal requirements.

    13. Changes to This Privacy Policy


    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
